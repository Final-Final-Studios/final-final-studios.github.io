About the Game

On the shores of Penglai, the Isle of Immortals, twelve legendary guardians stand between you and immortality. Every fight is a puzzle. Every death a lesson. Every encounter is a boss battle drawn from the Shanhaijing, China's ancient bestiary of myths and monsters. Some guardians demand vertical traversal. Others require manipulating the arena itself. Penglai comes to life through painterly ink-wash visuals inspired by traditional Chinese brush painting.

Key Features

  • 12 unique bosses: The Four Evils, the Dragon Kings, and the Gods of Mt. Kunlun.
  • Combat as puzzle: Observation, pattern recognition, and precise execution.
  • An island of secrets: Ruins, shrines, and echoes of ancient myth.
  • Ink wash art style: Every frame rendered as a living Chinese brush painting.
  • Challenge Mode & Leaderboards: Boss variants, mastery runs, global rankings.

Studio

Final Final Studios LLC is an independent game development studio founded by Xin and Mark Sullivan, two industry veterans with a combined 26+ years of experience and 11 shipped games spanning Harmonix, Google, and the MIT Game Lab. The studio was established to create premium, tightly crafted indie games that pair deep mechanical design with bold artistic vision.

  • Mark Sullivan — Code & Engineering. Former lead engineer at Harmonix. Shipped Rock Band, Disney Fantasia, Hasbro DropMix.
  • Xin Xin — Art & Design. Former senior designer at Google. Film & animation background. Leads art direction and the ink-brush visual style.
