Pitch

One small rabbit. Twelve ancient guardians. Immortal's Way is a boss-focused action game rooted in Chinese mythology. Study their patterns, strike with precision, and explore Penglai, the Isle of Immortals.

Key Features

  • 12 unique bosses drawn from the Shanhaijing — the Four Evils, the Dragon Kings, and the Gods of Mt. Kunlun.
  • Combat as puzzle: Every encounter demands observation, pattern recognition, and precise execution.
  • An island of secrets: Explore the sacred Isle of Immortals, uncovering ruins, shrines, and echoes of ancient myth.
  • Ink wash art style: Every frame rendered as a living Chinese brush painting.
  • Challenge Mode & Leaderboards: Boss variants, mastery runs, and global rankings for players who chase perfection.

Developer

Final Final Studios is a 2-person indie game studio based in California, USA. Our team has shipped multiple successful titles (Rock Band, Fantasia: Music Evolved, Black Hat Cooperative). The team brings 26+ years of combined industry experience from Google, Harmonix, and MIT Game Lab.
